CARDI B IS SCARED TO SHARE PICTURES OF HER DAUGHTER KULTURE KIARI CEPHUS AND HERES WHY

Cardi B has her reasons for not showing off her daughter, Kulture Kiari Cephus, online. The rapper tells ET Online that trolls make her nervous to share candids of her little one.

“I’m scared of sharing her to the world,” Cardi says of uploading photos of her and husband Offset’s daughter. “There are too many mean people out there,” the rapper continues. “Sometimes I do want to show people how beautiful and how precious she is,” Cardi gushes. “She’s so precious. There’s a lot of people that have crazy minds, you know. I want to protect her! She’s my little buggy.”

Kulture has been getting all of the love from mom and dad at home. Offset even goes where some men do not tread, which is the area of changing diapers. “He is changing diapers, but I’ve been seeing Offset being a dad,” Cardi B tells ET. “He already has three kids, so I already fell in love with the way that he was a dad already. I’m like, ‘Oh my god, I can have a little one with this guy.’ He’s really good.”

Cardi B and Offset have one child together. Will there be another little one to join the family in coming years? “I want two more,” Cardi B reveals. “It could always be a hit or miss, but I don’t want nothing right now.”

Keke Palmer Wows in Green Lace Dress at Nope UK Premiere with Daniel Kaluuya &amp; Brandon Perea

The stars of Jordan Peele‘s new movie Nope have arrived in London for the next stop of their press tour!

Keke Palmer, Daniel Kaluuya, and Brandon Perea hit the red carpet for the UK premiere of their new horror film on Thursday night (July 28) at the Odeon Luxe Leicester Square in London, England.

The three stars were joined by Donna Mills, who plays the role of Bonnie Clayton in the movie.

Meet all of the stars of the film in our cast list post and find out if there’s going to be a sequel to the hit movie.

After five days in theaters, the movie has grossed $58.4 million at the domestic box office.

FYI: Keke is wearing a Valentino dress and Jimmy Choo shoes.

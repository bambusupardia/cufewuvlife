Chase Stokes &amp; Madison Bailey Meet Up With Addison Rae for American Eagles Cozy Toether. Holid

Chase Stokes and Madison Bailey hit up the Cozy Together. Holiday Forever shopping event held at American Eagle Soho on Friday (December 3) in New York City.

The two Outer Banks stars were joined by TikTok and He’s All That star Addison Rae inside the event, where the trio met with fans and shopped around the holiday collection from the shop.

Madison and Chase, who are ambassadors to the brand, were spotted checking out the cozy sweaters, and sizing up Chase for a pair of new jeans.

Later on, Chase took a moment to check out the winter season beanies.

Meanwhile, Addison spotted the Holiday Faves display, and had her eye a few of the band tees there.

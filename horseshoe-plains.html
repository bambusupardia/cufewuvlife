Horseshoe Plains (Japanese: 蹄鉄ヶ原 Horseshoe Meadow) is an area located in the northern region of the Obsidian Fieldlands of Hisui. It connects to Aspiration Hill to the west.

Rei/Akari leads the player into this area during Mission 4: "Getting to Work on Research Tasks".

Geography

Horseshoe Plains is a large area that extends eastward from the base of Aspiration Hill. The plain is marked by several U-shaped lakes. A river curves around the eastern and southern end of the field from up north, acting as a natural border and preventing players without a Ride Pokémon from crossing. The southeastern tip of the plain connects to Worn Bridge, though the structure is too steep to climb without Sneasler.

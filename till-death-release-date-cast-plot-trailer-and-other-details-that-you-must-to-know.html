Till Death: Release Date, Cast, Plot, Trailer, And Other Details That You Must To Know!

Millennium Films is producing a horror thriller movie titled, Till Death, in which star Megan Fox plays the protagonist’s role. This is the first time since 2009, after starring in the movie Jennifer’s Body, that Megan Fox will be acting in another horror movie. The film focuses on marriage and hate, or better put, a marriage that went wrong. In this case, both partners are trying to kill each other off in a deserted place.

The movie is written by Jason Carvery, produced by David Leslie Johnson-McGoldrick, Yariv Lerner, and Tanner Mobley, it is directed by Dale S.K, in his directorial debut. The movie promises to be worth the money. 

This is not the only movie with the name, Till Death. There is a different movie of the same name, which was expected to release this year, but has also been shifted due to the coronavirus pandemic. The other Till Death movie is a love story featuring Evangeline Lily and Ted Lasso’s Jason Sudeikis.

It is hoped that one of the two movies drop the title.

Till Death Release Date: When Will it Premiere?

The movie was one of the first movies to be filmed during the coronavirus pandemic. Production was entirely done in Bulgaria, where there was strict adherence to the Covid-19 safety protocols. The filming took a month, between August 5 and September 12, and is now in post-production. However, the movie should be expected on July 1, 2021.

Till Death Plot: What will Happen?

The official movie plotline reveals that a woman, Emma (played by Megan Fox), is left chained to the body of her dead husband. The move is part of a deranged revenge plot, and following this act, there are hired assassins on the way to finish the job of killing her.

Millennium Films executive, Jeffrey Greenstein, was quoted as saying, “when the concept of the movie was released on Blood List, a funny and anxious ridden show that will surprise and excite viewers, we decided to make the movie.”

Till Death Cast: Who Can be in it?

There is no excellent information about the cast and actors in the upcoming movie, aside from the fact that we know that Megan Fox will be the protagonist starring as Emma. All other cast members don’t have an assigned role yet.

However, we would get to see these actors in the movie.

Callan Mulvey (Rush), Eoin Macken (Merlin), Aml Ameen (Sense8), and Jack Roth (Rouge One: A Star Wars Story).

Till Death Trailer: When can we watch it?

There is no trailer for the upcoming movie yet. However, we do expect a trailer to be released soonest.

The Relic Vase (Japanese: こだいのツボ Ancient Vase) is a valuable item introduced in Generation V.

In the core series games

Price

In the Generation V games, the Relic Vase can be sold to the billionaire maniac in Undella Town for $50000.

Effect

The Relic Vase serves no practical purpose other than to be sold.

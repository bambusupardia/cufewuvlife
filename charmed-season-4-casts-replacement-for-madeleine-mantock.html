Charmed Season 4 Casts Replacement for Madeleine Mantock!

There’s a new star coming to Charmed!

Following the exit of Madeleine Mantock, who announced she was leaving the series back in July, a new star has been cast as the new “Charmed One” in The CW’s reboot of the series, which stars Melonie Diaz, Sarah Jeffery, Rupert Evans and Jordan Donica.

Click inside to read more…

New showrunners Jeffery Lieber, Nicki Renna and Joey Falco revealed, via Variety, that Lucy Barrett will be taking over the role, and is described as: “An artist, with a wild, unpredictable love for life and a one-of-a-kind view of the world that serves her well in her art, which is her life’s passion. She’s fun, irreverent, impulsive, strong-willed, and will bring brand-new energy to the Power of Three.”

Her name, backstory, powers and connection to Mel and Maggie are not being revealed just yet.

In Season 4, Mel and Maggie find their lives transformed by the death of their beloved sister, Macy. But when their grief threatens to undo the Power of Three forever, the surprising arrival of a third Charmed One (Barrett) gives them a renewed opportunity to fulfill their destinies. And this couldn’t come at a better time, because a mysterious new enemy is rising in the shadows, instilling fear in every corner of the magical world. But as this new foe draws the Charmed Ones and their allies Harry (Evans) and Jordan (Donica) deeper into a twisted game, will this new trio be ready for what’s to come?

Lucy has starred in Co-Ed, The Wrong Girl, Bloom and Neighbors.

“Life’s good, let’s eat,” she said on social media upon the reveal of the casting.

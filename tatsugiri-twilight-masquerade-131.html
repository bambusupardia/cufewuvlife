Tatsugiri (Japanese: シャリタツ Syaritatsu) is a Dragon-type Basic Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Twilight Masquerade expansion.

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card and a Full Art illustration rare card in the Twilight Masquerade expansion, first released in the Japanese Transformation Mask expansion. The Regular print features artwork by Jerky, while the Full Art illustration rare print features artwork by osare.

An SVP Black Star Promotional print of this card with new artwork by Shimaris Yukichi was one of the Prerelease promos for Twilight Masquerade, available in the Twilight Masquerade Build & Battle Box. A print with a gold foil "Staff" stamp was given to those overseeing Prerelease Tournaments. A Non Holofoil version of the Twilight Masquerade card was also included in the Twilight Masquerade Build & Battle Box.

Trivia

Origin

This card's Regular print and SV-P Promotional print feature Tatsugiri in its Curly Form, while the Full Art illustration rare print features Tatsugiri in its Curly, Droopy, and Stretchy Forms. Surf is a move in the Pokémon games that Tatsugiri can learn via TM. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Violet.

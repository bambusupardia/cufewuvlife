East & West coast Shellos taken up a notch!

I definitely expect at least a few Kalos Pokémon to get variants in SV. PLA showed that they’re ready to tackle Gen 6 with Goodra and Avalugg. I expect them to put Unova in the backseat and focus on gens 2, 3, and 6.

I’m iffy on Gen 4, even though it has zero variants (I think), because all the Sinnoh Pokémon just got so much love with the last two games. It’s the same reason we haven’t seen Gen 7 variants- not enough time has passed since their games.

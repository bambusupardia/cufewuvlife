Picnic Basket (Japanese: ピクニックバスケット Picnic Basket) is an Item card. It is part of the Scarlet & Violet expansion.

Card text

Heal 30 damage from each Pokémon (both yours and your opponent's).
You may play any number of Item cards during your turn.

Trivia

Origin

Picnics are a feature in Pokémon Scarlet and Violet that allow the player to care for their Pokémon in various ways.

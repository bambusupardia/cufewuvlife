Kim Kardashian Reacts to North West Holding Up STOP Sign in Middle of Fashion Show (Video)

North West wasn’t in the mood for photographs while sitting front row at a fashion show this week in Paris and her mom Kim Kardashian is now reacting to the viral moment.

While attending the Jean Paul Gaultier show with Kim, Kris Jenner, and Cici Bussey, nine-year-old North was seen holding up a sign that said “STOP.”

The moment was caught on video and Kim shared the video on her Instagram account along with a reaction.

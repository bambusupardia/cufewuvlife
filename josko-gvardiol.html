Joško Gvardiol Biography

Josko Gvardiol was born in Zagreb, Croatia on January 23, 2002. He is the son of Tihomir Gvardio, an amateur footballer in his hometown of Novigrad.

Gvardiol began playing football at the age of seven with Zagreb’s Nogometni Klub Trenjevka. He subsequently transferred to the Dinamo Zagreb academy, where he was used as a left-back or center defender until coach Dalibor Poldruga shifted him to the center-back position.

Is Spartan Locke Dead? What Happened To Halo Infinites Spartan Locke? How Tall Is He?

Is Spartan Locke Dead?  Halo Infinite’s Spartan Locke is said to be dead.

Spartan Jameson Locke, a key character in the Halo series, has mysteriously vanished in the latest installment, Halo Infinite. Gamers have a few theories as to why this is.

What Happened To Halo Infinite’s Spartan Locke?

Netizens think Spartan Locke of Halo Infinite has died. Thus, many fans wonder where they missed him and how the story took a turn where he was no more seen.

He was one of the main characters in the game. In Halo season 5, Master Chief was discovered by the Guardians, Spartan Locke, and Fireteam Osiris, who confirmed that he is not a traitor.

Unfortunately, they’ve already been put in a worse predicament. Cortana has returned, she is a traitor, and she and her AI companions have launched a war on the universe.

Later, the Infinity is involved in a combat engagement in the orbit of a Halo ring, where Halo Infinite begins. In the Infinite series, there is hardly any physical appearance of Spartan Locke.

There is one mention to him in the form of a UNSC Audio Log collectible. Dr. Halsey suggests sending Locke to Zeta Halo to remove Cortana until Chief rejects and accepts the assignment.

Is Spartan Locke Dead?

Some people have speculated that Spartan Locke is probably dead in the Halo series. However, there is no concert evidence to prove such rumors. 

However, many think the creators may not continue his storyline following this series. It may be saddening for many of his fans.

Big Brother VIP finalists revealed:Ellie Gonsalves,Josh Carroll andLuke Toki

The Big Brother VIP grand finale will air on Tuesday night. 

And on Monday, the final three housemates who will compete for the win and $100,000 for their chosen charity were revealed. 

Actress Ellie Gonsalves, model Josh Carroll and Australian Survivor star Luke Toki are going head to head to take the prize. 

Finals: The Big Brother VIP grand finale will air on Tuesday night. Actress Ellie Gonsalves, model Josh Carroll and Australian Survivor star Luke Toki will go head to head to take the prize. All pictured 

The trio took their alliance to the end, after ousting Imogen Anthony during Monday’s episode.   

The housemates attacked their final challenge, with Josh securing the win and the power to decide which housemate would go home, as well as who he would take to the Grand Final with him. 

Imogen begged Josh not to vote for her so she could stay but her pleas fell on deaf ears. 

Grinners: They will compete for the win and $100,000 for their chosen charity 

On leaving the Big Brother VIP hotel, an emotional Imogen said, ‘I never thought I would make it this far. 

‘I did not expect to be so transformed by this experience, it’s definitely given me my spark back… and I feel like doing that, I can do almost anything.

‘I am excited for things ahead. I am not just someone’s ex, I am me, I am Immy, I am Imogen.’  

Vote: Tomorrow night on Big Brother VIP fans will vote who wins Big Brother’s game via a public vote. It won’t be a breeze reaching the top as Big Brother will have a few twists

Tomorrow night on Big Brother VIP fans will vote on who wins Big Brother’s game via a public vote.  

It won’t be a breeze reaching the top as Big Brother will have a few twists and turns on the menu before they check out. 

The Big Brother VIP Grand Final airs Tuesday at 7.30pm on Channel 7

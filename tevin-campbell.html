Who is Tevin Campbell:

Tevin Campbell is a famous R&B Singer. He was born on November 12, 1976 and his birthplace is Waxahachie, TX. Tevin is also well known as, American singer-songwriter and actor known for his hit single “Tell Me What You Want Me To Do.” His second album, I’m Ready, went double platinum. In 2016, he released a single titled “Safer On the Ground.”

Tevin is originated from United States. Quincy Jones is credited with introducing him to R&B music and jumpstarting his career.

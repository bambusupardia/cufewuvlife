William Thomas Hader Jr., popularly known as Bill Hader is an American who is a famous actor, writer, filmmaker, and comedian by profession. He is in the industry since the year 1999. He is the creator, producer, writer, sometimes director, and star of HBO’s dark comedy series Barry.

Bill Hader’s early breakthrough came during his eight-year term as a cast member on NBC’s long-running variety show Saturday Night Live (2005–2013), for which he won four Primetime Emmy Award nominations and a Peabody Award.

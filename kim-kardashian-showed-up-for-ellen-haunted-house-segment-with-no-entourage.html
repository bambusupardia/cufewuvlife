Kim Kardashian showed up for Ellen haunted house segment with no entourage

Kim Kardashian will soon be seen screaming her head off.

The reality star, 40, will be the final guest on Ellen DeGeneres’ “Haunted House” segment, in which a celeb takes on the titular attraction with the show’s scaredy-cat producer Andy Lassner.

A spy tells Page Six that the fright-fest was filmed at Universal Studios’ “Hollywood Halloween Horror Nights” attraction a few weeks ago and will air this Friday. The popular spot pulls in spooky figures from Universal’s IP holdings, including the “Halloween,” “Exorcist” and “Texas Chainsaw Massacre” franchises.

“People outside heard lots of screaming,” the spy continued, adding that security was tight shuffling the SKIMS founder in and out so news of her participation “wouldn’t leak.”

“Kim was really excited to do it,” the insider added, saying that “she came with almost nobody, no big entourage at all.”

Other celebs who have screamed their lungs out while dodging lunging zombies and the like have included Lizzo, Ariana Grande, “Modern Family” star Eric Stonestreet, Chrissy Teigen, and Sarah Paulson.

This is the final season for “Ellen” after 19 seasons. Before announcing the end of her eponymous chat-fest, DeGeneres was ensnared in several controversies after allegations arose last year that the set was a toxic workplace.

“As you may have heard, this summer there were allegations of a toxic work environment at our show and then there was an investigation,” DeGeneres said in a Season 18 monologue. “I take that very seriously and I want to say I am so sorry to the people that were affected … I take responsibility for what happens at my show.”
